What's the difference between B3 and B+?

Raven B+ is a fork of B3, with many new modules, and more configurability than the old B3. Raven B+ is being actively updated to suit any new requests for bypasses and modules. It has lots of new modules, which the old B3 didnt have, with improvments made to already existing ones, such as AutoClicker, or Scaffold.

Can I get a+ for free?

Ask the owners, and they'll decide whether you qualify, and it will only be if you have a semi-large youtube following. If you are just some random wanting free access, you will be denied.

How do I install?

For windows, once you have downloaded the .JAR and have forge installed, hold the windows key and "R" and a box will pop up. Type "%appdata%" and press the enter key. Go to .minecraft then mods folder and put the mod in there.

The free version of Raven B+ is fully open source, so feel free to view it on GitHub.
