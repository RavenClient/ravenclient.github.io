Useful Mods

Some Useful forge mods that can be paired with Raven B+ to improve your gameplay and experience! This can range from game optimizations to alt managers to just generally a better time playing Minecraft while using Raven B+. Click on the title of the mod to download, or scroll to the bottom and download them all! As Raven B+ updates, some mods might stop working, or cause the game to crash. This mods list is here as a guide and experimenting is needed!

Mod Downloads

Deluxe Sprint Better sprint mod. To edit keybind go to: Options > Controls > Scroll down and look for "Toggle Sprint".
Perspective Mod Allows you to rotate your view around your player, while not moving your players head. Like a better F5. To edit keybind go to: Options > Controls > Scroll down and look for "Perspective".
Hit Particles Shows sharpness particles and allows a variable intensity. To edit mod settings type: "/hitparticles" in chat.
Bedwars Stats Use "/bw [Players Name]" to check a players Bedwars starts while in a lobby.!
Powns Co-ords Shows your X, Y, Z and more, and displays it on your HUD. To edit mod settings type: "/coordshud" in chat.
Reach Display Shows how far your last hit was. To edit mod settings type: "/reachdisplaymod" in chat.
Powns Armour HUD Displays your armour on your HUD. To edit mod settings type: "/armourhud" in chat.
Powns Potions HUD Displays your current potion statuses on your HUD. To edit mod settings type: "/potionhud" in chat.
AutoGG Says "GG" after a Hypixel game. To edit mod settings type: "/autoGG" in chat.
BDSM Lite All round game improvments.
Optifine Better looking game, FPS improvments, Zoom and shaders (RECOMENDED MOD).
Patcher Patches some bugs in the game.
Player API This is a player API needed for some of these modules.
Sk1er's Old Animations Reverts block hitting animations to 1.7 and some other 1.8.9 animations back.
Vanilla Enhancements Improves gameplay, better FPS.
Time Changer Changes the time to whatever you want.
